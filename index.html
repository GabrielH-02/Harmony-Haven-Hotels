About Us & Our Story

Welcome to Harmony Haven Hotels! Our names are Alison and George and we spent the years of 2000 to 2004 traveling the globe full-time. Through our journeys, from bustling Asian markets to serene European landscapes, we understood the magnifying power of travel. Fuelled by our many adventures we embarked in creating Harmony Haven Hotels. Through our travels we have stayed in some of the most amazing hotels in the world as well as our fair share of bad ones too. A main contributing factor which made these decisions so difficult was price.

Therefore Harmony Haven Hotels is a hotel membership program, allowing young travellers to feel welcomed, and safe whilst making their own adventures. Spanning over 6 continents and 36 different hotels across the world, Harmony Haven Hotels has provided countless travellers with good quality stays and at constant price. With our two offers available you can explore and make your own special memories.
